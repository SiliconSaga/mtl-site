MTL Soccer uses **TeamSnap** to manage teams, rosters, schedules, and
communication. All parents and coaches should have the TeamSnap app installed.

## Setting Your Availability

**Please update your attendance for each session.** This helps your coach plan
the lineup and decide how to play in the event of a smaller attendance.

![Guide to using TeamSnap — set your availability for upcoming matches]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/teamsnap.png)

### How It Works

1. Open the TeamSnap app and find your **team**
2. Check the **Schedule** to see upcoming games
3. Tap **Set Availability** to mark whether your child will attend each session

We understand that players may not be available for every game. Setting your
availability in the app lets your coach decide how to manage the roster for
each session. We thank you in advance for your help with this.

## What Coaches Use TeamSnap For

- **Messaging your team** — send updates, reminders, and game-day details to
  all parents at once
- **Tracking rosters** — only players on the official TeamSnap roster should
  receive uniforms or participate in activities
- **Game schedules** — all games will be listed in the app with times, field
  locations, and field assignments

## Getting Started

If you haven't received a TeamSnap invitation for your child's team, contact
your coach or email [MTLsoccer@gmail.com](mailto:MTLsoccer@gmail.com).
