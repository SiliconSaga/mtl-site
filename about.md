MTL Soccer is part of the Mountain Top League (MTL), an all-volunteer
organization which has served the children of West Orange, NJ since it was
founded in 1959.

## Our Program

MTL Soccer serves over 1,000 children across spring, summer, and fall seasons.
We offer age-appropriate recreational soccer from pre-K through 6th grade:

- **Little Kickers** (ages 4-5) — introduction to soccer, focus on fun
- **1st/2nd Grade** (ages 6-8) — 4v4 play, learning positions
- **3rd/4th Grade** (ages 8-10) — 7v7 with goalies
- **5th/6th Grade** (ages 10-12) — 9v9 play

We also support **West Orange United Travel FC** for competitive play and
**TOPSoccer** as an adaptive program.

## Our Values

- **Sportsmanship** — learning to play fair, win graciously, and lose with dignity
- **Skill development** — age-appropriate coaching to build fundamentals
- **Fun** — every child should enjoy their time on the field

## Volunteer Organization

MTL Soccer is run entirely by volunteer coaches, referees, commissioners, and
trustees. We welcome new volunteers — no prior coaching experience required.

## Other MTL Sports

The Mountain Top League also offers baseball, basketball, hockey, and softball.
Visit [mountaintopleague.com](https://mountaintopleague.com/) for information
on all MTL sports.
