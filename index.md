MTL Soccer is part of the [Mountain Top League](https://mountaintopleague.com/),
an all-volunteer organization serving the children of West Orange, NJ since 1959.
Our recreational program serves over 1,000 children across spring, summer, and
fall seasons, emphasizing sportsmanship and a love for the game.
